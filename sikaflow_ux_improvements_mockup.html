Feature 1 — Digital Receipt & WhatsApp Sharing

After a sale, the customer can receive a digital receipt via SMS or WhatsApp. For MoMo payments, the system automatically sends the receipt to the payer's mobile number immediately on confirmation — no manual step required. For cash sales, the Field Agent can tap to share.

Feature 3 — Offline Training Videos

Training videos are accessible from the Menu tab and stored locally on the Sunmi device so they play without internet. Videos are pre-loaded on the device before deployment and can be updated when the device is online. Accessible from two entry points: Menu → Training, and a persistent "?" help button on the POS screen.

Implementation notes for Vietnam team
📱 Digital receipt — MoMo
Trigger SMS/WhatsApp send in the PSP/FI payment confirmed webhook, not in the app. Use the payer phone from the MoMo transaction object. Receipt link = sikaflow.app/r/{invoice_id}.
⚡ Auto new sale
After print callback fires, start a 3s Timer in Flutter. On tick, Navigator.pushReplacement to POS screen. Tapping "Stay on receipt" cancels the timer. Only trigger on successful print — not on print error.
🎓 Offline videos
Bundle videos as device assets pre-loaded by Trident Aliska before deployment. Use video_player Flutter package. Store metadata (title, duration, chapters, watched) in Drift SQLite. Show update banner when new versions detected on WiFi sync.
