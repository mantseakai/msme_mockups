A

Onboarding flow: sector → product pre-load

Triggered after registration is complete (20% profile). The system asks the trader to pick their sector, then shows a pre-loaded product list they can accept, edit, or skip. Designed to get traders to their first sale in under 3 minutes.

Implementation notes for Vietnam team

🗄️ Product template database

Create a product_templates table with columns: sector, name, default_price, category, unit, tax_type. Pre-seed with ~20 products per sector (8 sectors = ~160 rows). This is managed by Trident Aliska, not editable by traders. Templates are read-only; accepted products are copied into the MSME's own products table.

⚡ Trigger points

The sector-select + pre-load flow triggers in two places: (1) automatically after registration completes, shown as an optional onboarding step after the success screen. (2) from Inventory → Add new product → "Load suggested products". Both routes navigate to the same SectorPickerScreen → ProductSuggestionsScreen. Pass an isOnboarding: bool flag to adjust the heading and skip behaviour.

✏️ Inline edit

Each product row in the suggestions list is tappable. Tapping expands an inline editor showing name, selling price, and "what did you pay" fields. The default prices in the template are regional estimates, traders are expected to edit them to match their own prices. Editing is optional; tapping the checkbox and moving on is also fine.

📦 Stock entry step

After the trader confirms their selection, show one more screen with − / + steppers for opening stock quantity per product. This is optional (skippable). Products with stock = 0 are still created in the catalogue, the trader can restock later via Inventory. Writing stock at setup gets them to Group 3 (25% completion) immediately.

🔁 Skippable at every step

Every screen in this flow has a "Skip" / "Do this later" option in the top-right or below the primary CTA. If skipped at any point, the partial state is saved (e.g. if sector was chosen but products weren't confirmed, save the sector). The onboarding progress banner on the Home screen should show "Add products" as an incomplete step with a tap-to-resume link.

🏷️ Sector from registration

The MSME's sector is already captured at registration (Step 1 of the onboarding form). Where available, pre-select this sector automatically in the SectorPickerScreen and skip straight to the suggestions list. Only show the sector picker if the sector is missing or the trader wants to change it. Check msme.sector in the JWT or profile API response.
