Inclusive Design Principles Applied

🌍 Bilingual labels everywhere
Every button shows local language first, English second. Users learn at their own pace — the Twi/Ewe/Hausa/English toggle persists across all screens.
🎙️ Voice button on every screen
The blue mic FAB is always visible. Tap to speak in any language — the system reads totals, alerts and confirmations back aloud. No reading required.
👆 Large tap targets
All major actions are full-width or large-tile. No tiny links or small icons. The POS product grid uses emoji + price — recognisable even if you can't read.
🟠 Colour = meaning
Green = money/GRA. Orange = action/urgent. Blue = voice/info. Red badge = low stock. Users develop colour memory fast without relying on text.
📸 Next: product photos
In the real build, POS items will show the actual product photo taken with the Sunmi camera. A trader can tap the picture of oil vs typing "cooking oil".
🎙 Voice command examples (Twi)
"Rice bio" → adds Rice to cart
"Asɛde bɛn na mehia atua?" → reads tax due
"Ripɔt yi me" → generates & reads report
"Stok bɛn na wɔ ho?" → lists low stock items
